Comparison: Blast Furnace vs Midrex

Blast Furnace

  • • High temperature (1500–2000°C)
  • • Uses coke + hot air blast
  • • Produces molten pig iron
  • • High CO₂ emissions
  • • Indirect reduction via CO
  • • Slag produced

Midrex DRI

  • • Moderate temperature (~950°C)
  • • Uses natural gas reformed to H₂ + CO
  • • Produces solid DRI (sponge iron)
  • • Lower carbon footprint
  • • Both H₂ and CO as reductants
  • • No melting / no slag
