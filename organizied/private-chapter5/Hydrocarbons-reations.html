🧭 How to use

  • Click any compound → its box turns gold, every reaction in/out lights up, and full details appear here.
  • Click any arrow → the reaction name appears in large text right beside it, and only that reaction stays lit.
  • Route finder → choose two compounds in the toolbar and press Find path; the shortest conversion sequence glows step-by-step.
  • Legend pills → click a family (e.g. Halogen derivatives) to spotlight only that class.
  • 🎓 Quiz mode → hides all reagents/conditions on the arrows so you can test yourself; click again to reveal.
  • Zoom & pan → mouse wheel + drag, or the ＋ / － / ⊡ buttons.
